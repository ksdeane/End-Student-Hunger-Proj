Our goal

-------------------

--------------

Our ultimate goal is that every student, from Pre-k to 12th grade, is safe from food insecurity year-round.

--------------

There are many existing programs to help hungry kids, but there are not enough. As long as there is even 1 child (let alone 13 million children) going to school hungry and having little food assistence in the summer, our cause will always need support.

--------------

We hope to achieve our goal with the help of many awesome volunteers, donators, administrators, and legislators.
